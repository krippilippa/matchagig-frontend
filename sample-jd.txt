Technical Sales Representative – IT Staffing Startup (Remote)

About GigDeveloper:
We're a dynamic early-stage startup founded by a Swedish entrepreneur based in Mauritius, connecting skilled, English-fluent software developers from Mauritius and East Africa with tech startups and SMEs across Europe and the Middle East. With highly competitive rates, flexible contracts, and rapid onboarding, our solution is easy to sell and delivers exceptional value. As a truly global startup from day one, we fully embrace remote work and diverse international collaboration.

About the Role:
We’re looking for an energetic and driven Technical Sales Representative to help us build and expand our client base across Europe and the Middle East. This is a unique opportunity to join GigDeveloper at an early stage and play a key role in shaping the company's future. While sales is your primary focus, you’ll also have the chance to participate in broader startup activities, influence growth strategy, and enjoy the exciting hustle of building something from the ground up.

Key Responsibilities:
Identify and engage prospective clients (startups and SMEs) primarily through LinkedIn, referrals, networking, and outreach.
Clearly communicate GigDeveloper’s value proposition, including cost efficiency, skilled talent, flexible engagements, and fast onboarding.
Conduct initial client qualification calls to understand their staffing needs and project requirements.
Facilitate smooth communication between clients and internal teams during onboarding.
Regularly collaborate with internal stakeholders to ensure client satisfaction, retention, and growth.

Qualifications & Requirements:
Good verbal and written communication skills (English required).
Comfortable with remote work and managing your own time independently.
Proactive, self-motivated attitude, comfortable reaching out and connecting with new people regularly.
Basic familiarity with software development terms and IT technologies is strongly preferred.
Experience using LinkedIn and other social platforms to generate and nurture leads is beneficial.

Why Join Us?

Join a very early-stage startup, directly influence its success, and enjoy the hustle and excitement of startup life.
